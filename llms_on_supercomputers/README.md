# Large Language Models on Supercomputers

Jupyter Notebooks containing exercises & lectures for the [Foundations of LLM Mastery](https://events.asc.ac.at/category/4/) training series D1 - D3 of AI Factory Austria AI:AT and Austrian Scientific Computing (ASC) – course material development started in 2024 within EuroCC Austria and was handed over to AI Factory Austria AI:AT in autumn 2025. Adopted for use with Bazzite.ai from [https://gitlab.tuwien.ac.at/vsc-public/training/LLMs-on-supercomputers](https://gitlab.tuwien.ac.at/vsc-public/training/LLMs-on-supercomputers).

**Contents – Foundations of LLM Mastery:**

- D1 – Prompt Engineering Essentials
- D2 – Retrieval Augmented Generation
- D3 – Fine-tuning on one GPU
